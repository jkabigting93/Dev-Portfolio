Joey is a newly-graduated developer from the UofT SCS Coding Boot Camp. Growing up in Toronto, Canada, Joey graduated from the University of Toronto in 2015 with an Hon. BSc. in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology. He then completed his Masters of Forest Conservation with the U of T Faculty of Forestry, which culminated in a capstone thesis on invasive species growth in Toronto's urban ravine systems.

Seeking to bridge some skills gaps for his career goals, Joey completed a full-stack coding boot camp at U of T. This course began with the HTML/CSS/Javascript basics, which eventually led to the MERN (Mongo/Express/React/Node) stack. He is now looking for a role as a junior developer, preferably in the environmental or social sectors.

When not coding, Joey enjoys hiking, rock climbing, food blogging, loud concerts, and spoiling his dog.
